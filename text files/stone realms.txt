het stenen rijk / de stenen rijken

what stone realms is not:
* not a renderer
* not an idle game
* not an mmo
* not a serious fantasy game

stone realms is:
* a web rpg with buttons, menus
* where you travel on the overmap
* a mobile-friendly low-time-investment game

the gameplay loop of stone realms:
* travel costs points or energy but arrival is instant
* each location has different things to do
